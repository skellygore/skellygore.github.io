  • Zophar's Domain for most video game music in its original format
  • Modarchive for tracker music
  • Beatmania files
  • vgmrips.net, specifically for the .vgm format from the sega genesis, may have video game music that's been converted to .vgm format such as pc-98 touhou making it easier to split stems for than with the original pc-98 files
